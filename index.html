Letter Match

  1. Enter your name in the blue input field.
  2. Click the 'Start Game' button.
  3. When the game has started, you will see a letter from the alphabet.
  4. Find the letter that matches this letter in the letter row.
  5. In 30 seconds, try to find as many matches as possible.
  6. You can find the amount of your correct answers in the bottom of the game.
